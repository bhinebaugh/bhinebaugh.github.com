It's cephalopod week! Cephalopods are molluscs, but quite unlike the clam or snail variety. These carnivorous marine animals feature the largest brains of any invertebrates, and are capable of moving by jet propulsion.

Physically, cephalopods are distinguished by a beak, tentacles, and blue blood (copper-based hemocyanin).
