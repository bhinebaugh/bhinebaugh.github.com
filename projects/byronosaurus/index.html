Byronosaurus jaffei

Byronosaurus was a theropod dinosaur in the troodontid family from the Late Cretaceous.

The first fossil remains of this species were discovered in 1993 in Mongolia. (Similar to many maniraptorans like Troodon and Velociraptor) It was feathered, had a retractable sickle-claw on the second toe. many unserrated teeth, unlike most troodontids, more like Archaeopteryx It probably ate small mammals and lizards.
